## Notes on Disclosure
The improvised structural supports (tree trunks, unsecured pipe columns) and the bathroom hole are material defects. Michigan requires seller disclosure of known material defects. If the executor or listing agent was aware of these and they were not disclosed, there is potential legal exposure — which further incentivizes the estate to accept a realistic offer rather than attract a buyer who discovers these post-close and sues.
